# Amazon-India-Sales-Dashboard
Data analytics project using Power BI and Excel Power Query to analyze Amazon sales in India

OBJECTIVE: 
To analyse Amazon’s sales performance across product categories, regions, and customer behaviour for the year 2025 in INDIA. Also, to derive actionable insights that can 
improve business outcomes. 
INTRODUCTION: 
The Amazon India Sales Dataset 2025 contains 15,000 e-commerce transactions designed to simulate real-world sales on Amazon India.It captures customer purchasing behaviour, product details, payment preferences, and feedback patterns across various product categories throughout the year 2025. This report is based on a comprehensive dashboard capturing Amazon’s sales data for 2025. It includes metrics such as total sales, order volume,customer distribution, product performance, and delivery status. The goal is to uncover patterns in consumer behaviours, regional demand, and operational efficiency. 

KEY FINDINGS AND INSIGHTS: 

Sales Overview 
 Total Sales: ₹1 billion 
 Total Orders: 45,000 
 Total Customers: 15,000 
 Average Sales per Order: ₹75,000 
 Average Rating: 3.04 
 Sales gradually increases from Quarter 1 to Quarter 4 of the year. 
 February marks the month with least sales and august with most sales. 

Top Categories and Products 
 Categories: Beauty (₹227.49M), Electronics (₹226.56M), Books (₹225M),Clothing(₹222.41M), Home & Kitchen (₹216.7M) 
 Products: Lipstick, Hair Dryer, Headphones, Sneakers. But there is seasonal change in the product demands like the jackets sales increase during the January. 

Regional Performance 
 The total sales analysis shows North east region leads and central region ranks least. 
 In the year 2025 more customers are from Sikkim (576) while least are from Andhra Pradesh (462).  

Order & Delivery Insights 
 Return Rate: 33% (high and concerning) 
 Delivery Status: Delivered (5,075), Returned (4,881), Pending (5,044) 
 Payment Methods: Card, Cash on Delivery, UPI. Most people go for card payment (45.4%). 

Customer Experience 
 Highest Customer Ratings: Tripura, Haryana, Andhra Pradesh. 
 Lowest Customer Ratings: Arunachal Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Meghalaya. 

SUGGESTIONS: 
Marketing Optimization-Focus campaigns on high-performing categories and products to maximize Return on Investment.
Logistics Improvement-Address the high return rate by improving packaging, delivery timelines, and product quality checks. 
Customer Experience Enhancement-Target low-rated states with better support, feedback collection, and localized service improvements. 
Inventory Planning-Align stock levels with regional demand trends to reduce overstock and stockouts. 

CONCLUSION 
The 2025 Amazon sales data reveals strong category performance and regional growth, butalso highlights operational challenges like high return rates and uneven customer satisfaction. By acting on these insights, Amazon can enhance its customer experience, streamline operations, and drive future growth.
